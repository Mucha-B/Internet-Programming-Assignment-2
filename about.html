About TechTrend

TechTrend was founded in 2021 in Nairobi's thriving innovation district — commonly known as Silicon Savannah. What started as a small WhatsApp group of tech journalists and software engineers has grown into one of East Africa's most trusted technology publications.

Today we cover everything from mobile money innovation (like M-Pesa) to AI, agritech, and the continent's booming startup ecosystem. Our stories are read by developers, founders, students, and policy makers across Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, and beyond.

"Africa is not just adopting technology — we are inventing the future. Our job is to tell that story."

Our Values

  • Accuracy first: Every claim is verified by at least two independent sources before we publish.
  • Plain language: We avoid unnecessary jargon so that any reader can follow along.
  • Independence: We do not accept payment to alter our editorial coverage.
  • Global perspective: Technology affects everyone, and our coverage reflects that.
  • Curiosity: We ask "why" and "how" before we ask "what".
